  • Long-term business goals

About

Stephanie Louie
Founder and Chief Strategist
An entrepreneur at heart, I created One10 North to ensure that every brand has the chance to succeed.
Based in San Francisco, we are a brand and design consultancy that leverages customer and consumer insights to define a brand's purpose and create strategic plans to help every business reach their North Star.
Working together, my team of strategists, researchers, designers and innovators bring the top-notch support and strategic guidance that many of the big brands enjoy. However, we do so at a fraction of the cost by eliminating the "fluff" and focusing on what's really needed to deliver meaningful results.
And, we can lead a project from beginning to end, or step in at any stage in the process. Our approach is tailored to what works for you, and we have one goal in mind - to help you succeed and find your North Star.
